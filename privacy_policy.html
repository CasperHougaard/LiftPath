Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our Fitness Training App ("the App"). This App is designed for users aged 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

2. Data Collection and Storage

2.1 Local Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device only. The App does not transmit, share, or synchronize any information to external servers or third-party services.

The following information may be stored on your device:

  • Workout Data: Exercise names, sets, weights, repetitions, dates, notes, and ratings
  • Exercise Library: Custom exercise names and categories you create
  • Training Sessions: Complete workout history including dates and all logged sets
  • App Settings: Rest timer preferences, progression settings, and user preferences

This data is stored in a local JSON file on your device's internal storage and is only accessible by the App itself.

3. Permissions and Usage

The App requests the following permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To display rest timer notifications during workouts. Used to show countdown timers and completion alerts.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE: To run a rest timer service that provides continuous countdown visible to users even when the app is in the background. This ensures accurate timing between exercise sets.
  • VIBRATE: To provide haptic feedback when rest timer completes. Enhances user experience by alerting users when rest period ends.

All permissions are used solely for the stated purposes and are not used to collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transmit any user data. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No data is shared with third-party services
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertising networks are integrated
  • No cloud synchronization occurs

5. Data Security

Your data is stored in the App's private directory on your device, protected by Android's app sandbox security model. Only the App itself can access this data. We recommend:

  • Using device lock screen protection
  • Regular device backups if you want to preserve your workout history
  • Using the App's built-in export feature (if available) to create manual backups

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your workout data remains on your device until you:

  • Delete the App (which removes all stored data)
  • Use the "Reset Data" feature within the App's Settings
  • Clear the App's data through your device's system settings

You have full control over your data. If you delete the App, all associated data is permanently removed from your device.

7. Age Restrictions

This App is designed for users aged 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you are under 18, please do not use this App. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

8. User Rights

As a user of this App, you have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the App (visible within the App)
  • Modify or delete any data at any time
  • Export your data (if export feature is available)
  • Delete all data by using the Reset Data feature or uninstalling the App

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the App's data handling, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing for this App.

11. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - for EU users
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) - noting that the App is for users 18+
  • Google Play Developer Policy requirements

This Privacy Policy applies only to the Fitness Training App and does not cover any third-party websites, services, or applications that may be linked or referenced.
